Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tools

Is this really free?

Yes! All our tools are 100% free to use with no hidden costs. We believe powerful image processing tools should be accessible to everyone.

Are my images uploaded to your servers?

No. All processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP for input. For output, you can choose between JPG, PNG, WebP, and PDF formats.

Do your tools work on mobile?

Absolutely! Our tools are fully responsive and work on any device - smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend files under 20MB for best performance, but you can process larger files depending on your device's capabilities.

How long are my processed files saved?

We don't save your files at all. Your processed images are available for immediate download but are not stored on any server.
